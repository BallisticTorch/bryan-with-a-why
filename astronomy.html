Why I Ask

Astronomy is the oldest science—and still the most mysterious. I ask:

  • Why is the speed of light the cosmic speed limit?
  • Why do black holes warp time and space?
  • Why is dark matter invisible yet everywhere?
  • Why do galaxies form spirals?
  • Why does the universe seem fine-tuned for life?
